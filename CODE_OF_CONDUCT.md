# Code of Conduct

This gallery exists to show off what's possible with VectoJS. To keep
discussion in issues welcoming:

- Be respectful — critique the code, not the person.
- No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory content.

Maintainers may close issues, lock threads, or (in serious or repeated
cases) block a participant to enforce this. If you experience or witness
behavior that violates this, open an issue or contact a maintainer
directly.
