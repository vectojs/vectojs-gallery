# Code of Conduct

This gallery exists so people can show off what they've built and discover
what's possible with VectoJS. To keep that welcoming:

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, and reviews — critique the code, not the person.
- No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory content, in submissions or in
  discussion.
- No malicious, offensive, or misleading creations — see
  [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for the specific technical rules
  (no network access, VectoJS-only imports, etc.).
- Assume good faith. Most rejected PRs are rejected for a technical reason
  (see the PR checklist), not a judgment about the contributor.

Maintainers may close PRs, lock threads, or (in serious or repeated cases)
block a contributor to enforce this. If you experience or witness behavior
that violates this, open an issue or contact a maintainer directly.
